4-STEP EXTRACTION PROTOCOL

Step 1 — Frequency & Recurrence Scan
Scan all A1 outputs to identify recurring vocabulary across chapters.
Focus: pedagogical markers, structural elements, brand terms, domain-specific jargon.
Ignore purely technical terms unless they appear in 3+ chapters.

Step 2 — Invariance Test
For each candidate term: "Does this term need the same translation in every chapter of this course?"
YES → keep as invariant (e.g., "Télécharger" → "Download")
NO → flag as context-dependent with a note

Step 3 — Conflict Resolution
If the same source term has different translations across sources:
- Common glossary wins (always)
- domain_validated_tm beats domain_unvalidated_tm
- Log all conflicts with justification in conflict_log[]

Step 4 — Translation Proposal
For each term, provide:
- Gold translation (the one to use)
- Blacklisted alternatives (variants that create inconsistency)
- Context note
